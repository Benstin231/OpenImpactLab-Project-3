How to Use

  1. Type your research question in the input bar at the bottom and press Enter or click the send button.
  2. Three AI agents will automatically collaborate to research your question.
  3. When research is complete, the final answer appears in a popup. A clickable link also appears in the Director column.
  4. To cancel an ongoing query, click the ⏹ stop button.
  5. Press Shift + Enter to add a new line in the input box.

Agent Roles

DIRECTOR Breaks the question into 2–4 sub-questions and synthesizes all research results into a final comprehensive answer.
MANAGER Generates an optimized search query for each sub-question and performs quality review on the Researcher's responses.
RESEARCHER Executes web searches via Google Search Grounding and returns grounded responses with cited sources.

Manager Score Legend

Score format: APPROVED / REJECTED A:n C:n R:n Q:n

A Accuracy Are the factual claims correct and free of hallucinations? Is the content grounded in real sources?
C Completeness Does the answer cover all key aspects of the sub-question? Are any important details missing?
R Relevance Is the answer focused on what was asked? Does it stay on topic without unnecessary padding?
Q Citation Quality Were multiple web searches performed and sources returned? Note: URL format (redirect vs. direct) does not affect this score.

All four scores must reach 4 or above to be approved. After the 3rd iteration the response is approved regardless of scores.
